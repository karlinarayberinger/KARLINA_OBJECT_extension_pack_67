Whether or not the following is true, I would like to just say it:

A part of me will probably always be a misanthrope.

Maybe deep down I think I’m the only person who deserves to exist (and that I should be serviced by robots and such that I maximize my longevity and happiness with their help).

I thought society would ignore me more than it apparently does. Perhaps people think I have too much potential to serve them to be thrown away (while they justify torturing me into compliantly serving them and appearing to enjoy it).

I don’t enjoy being imprisoned, censored, tortured, lied to, stolen from, and assaulted. I could only apparently be happy if I do enjoy those things.

I conclude I will most likely be too unhappy with how people treat me for me to voluntarily spend time with them.

I don’t want to make humans the gatekeeper to my happiness.

I think too many humans too often are too intentionally disrespectful of my boundaries.

I’m boycotting friendship with humans (but not smooth talking and play-acting to influence the apes’ behavior in service to my personal agenda).
